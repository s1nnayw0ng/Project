Hidden Waters

Dive in the hidden waters..

Swimming in open water offers the opportunity to connect with nature, nourish creativity, garner spiritual inspiration and experience the sublime. Wild Swimming takes place anywhere that isn’t a swimming pool. This means no man-made sides and bottom, and no lane ropes or black lines to follow. Most people find the freedom and lack of chemicals so invigorating they can’t help coming out of the water feeling happy and relaxed.
